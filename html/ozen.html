Biography
" A phantom of a memory, Omen hunts in the shadows. He renders enemies blind, teleports across the field, then lets paranoia take hold as his foe scrambles to learn where he might strike next."

Omen is a shadowy controller who strikes from the unknown. He uses Shrouded Step to teleport, Paranoia to blind, and Dark Cover to block vision. His ultimate, From the Shadows , lets him teleport anywhere

Abilities

Dark Cover

EQUIP a shadow orb, entering a phased world to place and target the orbs. HOLD FIRE while targeting to move the marker further away.

From the Shadows

EQUIP a tactical map. FIRE to begin teleporting to the selected location.

Paranoia

EQUIP a blinding orb. FIRE to throw it forward, briefly Nearsighting and Deafening all players it touches.

Shrouded Step

EQUIP a shrouded step ability and see its range indicator. fear. FIRE to begin a brief channel, then teleport to the marked location.
