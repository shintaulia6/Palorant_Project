VCT Ascended team are now available

Celebrate the Ascension champions with the latest team capsules.

Game Updates | 07/07/25

Champions Rise Again

The 2025 VCT season is in full swing, and it’s time to celebrate the best of the best! Ascended teams from around the globe are now officially part of the VCT Team Capsules collection. Get ready to show your support in style with exclusive cosmetics for the latest VCT challengers!

Introducing the Ascended Squads

Joining the elite are:

  • 2Game Esports (Americas)
  • Apeks (EMEA)
  • Xi Lai Gaming (CN)
  • Nongshim Redforce and BOOM Esports (Pacific)

These teams have earned their spot among the best—and now you can represent them in-game.

What's Inside the Team Capsule?

Just like the partnered team bundles released earlier this year, these capsules pack a punch:

  • Upgraded Classic Skin: Custom-designed to reflect each team's unique style.
  • Achievement Tracker Inspect: See major victories and milestones right in-game.
  • 2025 Player Card: Premium visuals showcasing team identity and creative flair.
  • Spray & Gun Buddy: Let everyone know where your allegiance lies!

Where to Find Them

You can grab the 2025 Ascended Team Capsules from the Esports Store, available all season long. Whether you're cheering for a region or a favorite roster, now's your chance to rep your team on the battlefield.

Join the Celebration

From daring clutches to legendary comebacks, these teams have proven their place in the spotlight. Celebrate their ascent and get ready to deploy with pride!
