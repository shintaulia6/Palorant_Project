State of the Agents - March 2024

Coleman Palm and John Goscicki touch on balance and the first new Agent of 2024.

Game Updates | 03/01/24

Agent Balance - Coleman Palm

Hey all, I'm Coleman, one of the members of the live balance team. We wanted to check in and talk with you about the state of Palorant in terms of Agent balance, and shed some light on some of the changes we've shipped over the last few months.

Palorant's balance philosophy has always focused on a measured approach—giving changes time to settle so we can make informed decisions. However, in 2024, we're trying a more proactive approach, leading to updates for Agents like Cypher, Deadlock, Gekko, Skye, and Iso. Still, we're careful not to shift the meta too fast, especially with the Esports scene in mind.

Our main goal now is to improve playstyle diversity within each role. Recent changes focused on balancing Initiators like Skye and Gekko, and Sentinels like Deadlock, Cypher, and Chamber. That said, balance work will continue across roles throughout the year, though patch cadence may slow down to let teams adjust and provide space for feedback.

Our Next Agent - John Goscicki

You've heard from Coleman about the current state of balance and how we're thinking about things throughout the remainder of the year. But I'm here to give you some hints into our first new Agent of 2024…

Harbor, our last Controller, debuted in 2022. At that time we wanted to double down on large-area map control, given the size of our newer maps like Lotus.
