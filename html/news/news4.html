Introducing VCT 2025 Season Capsule!

The Season Capsule lets you support all VCT teams, with revenue shared equally across league.

Esports | 02/02/25

Today we want to introduce the all new VCT 2025 Season Capsule, a celebration of the competitive season and a new way to represent your region and home league in VALORANT! With the launch of this capsule, this season of the VCT will feature the largest amount of esports collections ever, giving players the most amount of options to support and contribute to the success of your favorite teams and leagues.

What's in the 2025 Season Capsule?

Here's what's included the 2025 Season Capsule:

  • VCT Karambit : A sleek new melee weapon and the first-ever skin with five chromas, representing the four International Leagues and the VCT theme. At Level 3, top fraggers will see a special glow.
  • 4 Regional Player Cards : Animated designs that celebrate the identity of each International League.
  • 4 Gun Buddies : Reactive charms that represent each region and activate when fired, adding flair to every match.

    • Supporting Teams, Driving Growth

    The Season Capsule is designed to showcase pride in your region and league, while supporting all VCT teams equally. Unlike Team Capsules, which highlight individual teams, the Season Capsule ensures fair revenue sharing across the board. It builds on the Champions Collection model, giving fans another way to support the VCT. After noticing large performance gaps in Team Capsule sales last year—mainly benefitting major winners—we created the Season Capsule to raise the baseline for all teams. In 2024, fans generated over $44M for partnered teams through cosmetics, and we aim to continue that momentum this season.

    When Can You Grab the Season Capsule?

    The 2025 Season Capsule will launch globally on February 6th at approximately 2pm PST in the in-game store and will remain available until the end of Masters Bangkok. Grab the VCT 2025 Season Capsule to represent and support your favorite esports teams!
