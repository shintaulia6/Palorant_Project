Biography
"Ghanaian Agent Astra harnesses the energies of the cosmos to reshape battlefields to her whim. With full command of her astral form and a talent for deep strategic foresight, she’s always eons ahead of her enemy’s next move."

Astra’s main gimmick is her ability to reshape the battlefield from afar using Astral Form. . She places Stars that can become Gravity Wells to pull enemies, Nova Pulses to concuss, or Nebulas to obscure vision. Her ultimate, Cosmic Divide , creates a massive wall that blocks bullets and sound, giving her unmatched control over space and timing.

Abilities

Cosmic Divide

ACTIVATE to enter Astral Form and place a Star. When Cosmic Divide is ready, press ALT SHOOT tin Astral Form to aim, then SHOOT to place two points.

Gravity Well

Place a Star in Astral Form (X) ACTIVATE one Star to form a Gravity Well. Players in the area are sucked into its center before the well explodes, rendering all players still trapped inside Vulnerable.

Nebula

ACTIVATE a Star to turn it into a Nebula (smoke). USE ta Star to Disappear, returning the Star to be placed in a new location after a short period of time.

Nova Pulse

ACTIVATE one Star to detonate a Nova Pulse.The Nova Pulse will charge for a moment, then strike, shaking all players in the area.
