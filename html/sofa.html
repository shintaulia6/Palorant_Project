Biography

Born from the eternal winter of the Russian tundra, Sova tracks, finds, and dispatches enemies with devastating efficiency and precision. His special bow and extraordinary reconnaissance abilities ensure that you can run, but you can't hide.

Abilities

Shock Bolt

EQUIP your bow with a shock bolt. SHOOT to send out an explosive bolt, detonating on impact and damaging nearby players. HOLD SHOOT to extend the projectile's range. ALT SHOOT to add up to two ricochets to this arrow.

Recon Bolt

EQUIP your bow with a recon bolt. SHOOT to fire a recon bolt forward. ALT SHOOT to add up to two ricochets to this arrow. HOLD SHOOT to extend the projectile's range.

Owl Drone

START the owl drone. SHOOT to deploy and control the drone's movement.

Hunter's Fury

READY the bow with three long-range, wall-piercing energy blasts. SHOOT to release a line of energy blasts in front of Sofa. REUSED up to two more times during the active ability timer.
