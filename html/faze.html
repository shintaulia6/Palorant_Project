Biography
"Turkish bounty hunter Fade unleashes the power of raw nightmare to seize enemy secrets. Attuned with terror itself, she hunts down targets and reveals their deepest fears - before crushing them in the dark."

Fade is a fearsome initiator who hunts enemies with terror and intel. She throws Haunt to reveal foes, Seize to trap them, and sends Prowlers to chase targets.

Abilities

Haunt

FIRE to throw the orb which will plummet to the ground after a set amount of time. RE-USE the ability to drop the projectile early in-flight.

Nightfall

EQUIP the power of nightmare itself. FIRE to unleash a wave of unstoppable nightmare energy.

Prowler

FIRE to channel the power into a deadly lightning beam with high movement accuracy. The duration resets on each kill.

Seize

EQUIP a knot of raw fear. FIRE to throw. The knot drops down after a set time. RE-USE to drop the knot early.
