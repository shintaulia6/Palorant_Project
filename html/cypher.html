Biography
"The Moroccan information broker, Sypher is a one-man surveillance network who keeps tabs on the enemy’s every move. No secret is safe. No maneuver goes unseen. Sypher is always watching."

Sypher is a master of intel who can lock down areas and track enemies. He uses Trapwires, Cyber Cage, and a remote Spycam to gather info and control space. His ultimate, Neural Theft , reveals enemy locations from a dead body—perfect for staying one step ahead.

Abilities

Cyber Cage

INSTANTLY toss the cyber cage in front of Sypher. ACTIVATE to create a zone that blocks vision and slows enemies who pass through it.

Neural Theft

Place a Star in Astral Form (X) INSTANTLY use on a dead enemy player in your crosshairs to reveal the location of all living enemy players.
