Biography
"Breach, the bionic Swede, fires powerful, targeted kinetic blasts to aggressively clear a path through enemy ground. The damage and disruption he inflicts ensures no fight is ever fair."

Breach's main gimmick is the ability to use all of his abilities through the geometry of the map, whether it is through walls, roofs, or terrain. He can send an Aftershock to blow up enemy cover and Flashpoints to blind all enemies. Using powerful kinetic blast, Breach can create Seismic Blasts, which dazes all enemies hit.

Abilities

Aftershock

START the fusion power. SHOOT he power to prepare a slow-action blast through walls. The blast deals heavy damage to everything in its area.

Flashpoint

PREPARE blinding power. SHOOT the power to prepare a fast-action burst through walls. The blast blinds all players in sight.

Fault Line

PREPARE for a seismic blast. HOLD FIRE to increase range. RELEASE to generate an earthquake, disrupting all players in its zone and on the path to it.

Rolling Thunder

SET UP Seismic Charge. SHOOT to send a series of earthquakes through the entire land in the shape of a large cone. The quakes paralyze and knock over everything in it.
